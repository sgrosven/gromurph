Java Score - Finish Dialog

This panel finishes boats or record mark roundings.  It has 3 main parts. 

Finish Table:  In the top left is a table that is analogous to most race committee's finish sheets.  You can enter a sail number in the sail column, a finish time in the finish column, and/or click on the penalty field to enter penalty information.

When you enter a time, you should use one of the following formats:  'hh:mm:ss',  'hhmmss', 'mmss', 'ss'.  When you omit hours or minutes, I'll automatically insert the last use hours and/or minutes.

Unfinished Table: In the bottom right is a list of unfinished boats.  Double clicking on any boat in this list will add that boat as the last finisher.

Current Entry/Finish: In the top right is a display-only panel showing the details of whatever boat/finish you last highlighted.  This could be a row in the finish table, or an entry in the unfinish list

Insert Button: Inserts a new finish slot at the row selected in the table.  First click on the row where you want the new finish placed.  Press Insert, the current finish in that row and all subsequent finishes will be moved down one row and an empty finish created for you to enter a sail number.  Note that if all boats are registered as finishers, you cannot insert... to MOVE a boat, first delete its old finish, then insert its new finish

Delete Button: Deletes a finisher, return the boat to the unfinished list.  Click on the finisher you wish to delete.  Press Delete 

Add Remaining button:  Provides a means for creating finishes for all remaining unfinished boats.  These finishes must have some "non-finish" penalty associated with them.  Click on the Add Remaining button, and you will be given a dialog with a button for each of the non-finish penalties.  Click on the desired penalty, and all of those boats will be moved to the finish table with that penalty assigned to them.  (NOTE: that you will be unable to score a race if there are boats that should have sailing that race that are not registered in the finish table.
